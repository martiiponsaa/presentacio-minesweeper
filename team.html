What We Do

Our goal is to offer a more personalized and educational Minesweeper experience. With our web app, players can not only enjoy the classic game but also improve their skills by:

  • Reviewing their own games to learn from past mistakes
  • Getting guidance from an AI assistant that explains game situations
  • Discovering their correct and incorrect moves
  • Watching games played by their friends
  • Tracking advanced statistics of their gameplay
